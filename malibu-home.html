Biography

Who Is Malibu June?

Malibu June is a band composed of four members. Peyton Jones is the singer of the band, and is known for his deep melodic voice. Presley Long is the keyboardist, and on bass guitar is Aidan Ratcliff. The drummer is TC Irons. The group has been playing together for several years now, however, this is their first public debut.

Peyton Jones - Lead Singer

Peyton is a down to the earth country boy, who in his free time enjoys spending time in nature. He spent much time in the woods of his home town, and he spent much time singing with the birds. He has become an excellent singer because of it and has a great voice.

Presley Long - Piano Player

Presley is a smart girl who plans to go to college for mathematics. As one of her extracurriculars, she began playing the piano. She was a fast learner and was capable of seeing the mathematical reasoning behind the music. She fell in love with the beauty of music, and has composed multiple pieces on the piano, however, she enjoys the different variations of sound that can be made via the keyboard.

Aidan Ratcliff - Bass Player, Guitarist

Aidan is a guitar player and artist. He enjoys the artistic freedom that comes with writing songs and playing the bass and guitar. He enjoys expressing himself through his art. He is also into body building and spends much time in the gym. To get a pump he listens to heavy metal, but to balance it out, he plays the amazing smooth rhythm found in the songs by Malibu June.

TC Irons - Drummer

TC is the life of the party for the team. His real name is Travis Crawford. He was born in Mexico, but was put up for adoption and was moved to South Carolina. He enjoys staying up late and partying, and being loud with his friends. TC loves playing the drums loud, but he slows it down a bit when he plays with the band.
